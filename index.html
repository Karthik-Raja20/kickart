Our story

WE BELIEVE EVERYONE DESERVES THE BEST DEAL ON THEIR SOLE.

WHO WE ARE

SoleMart is India's first intelligent shoe comparison platform. Founded in 2024, we solved one simple problem: why check 5 apps to find the best shoe deal? We aggregated it all in one place.

BRANDS WE SELL

We partner with authorized resellers of Nike, Adidas, Puma, Bata, Reebok, Converse, Skechers, Woodland, Liberty, New Balance, and more — 100% authentic, zero fakes guaranteed.

WHY TRUST US

Every product passes a 3-stage authentication process. Price data updates every 4 hours from live platform APIs. Our comparison table in wishlist and cart shows real-time data from Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra & Ajio.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Zero ads, zero sponsored placements, zero bias. The best deal always floats to the top. Combined with 24/7 support, easy replacement, and a premium UI — we're your shoe-buying partner for life.

HOW WE MATCH EXPECTATIONS

We built SoleMart around one core belief: every rupee matters. Our platform is designed so the best deal always wins. We're not just a store — we're a complete shoe intelligence platform that helps you make smarter decisions every time you shop. From our color-swatch card design to the live price comparison in your wishlist, every feature exists to save you money and time.

FAQ

How does price comparison work?
We fetch live prices from Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra & Ajio every 4 hours using their APIs. The best price is highlighted in your card, wishlist & cart automatically.
Are all products genuine?
Yes. Every brand undergoes a 3-stage authenticity check before listing. We partner only with authorized resellers. Zero fakes, ever.
How do I return a product?
Go to My Orders → Select item → Click Return. We'll arrange a free pickup within 24 hours. Refund is processed in 3–5 business days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders under ₹10,000.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery: 3–5 business days. Express delivery: 1–2 business days. Same-day delivery available in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore & Pune.
