Our Story

We Built This Because
We Got Tired of Overpaying.

It started with a simple frustration: buying the same Nike shoe on Amazon for ₹4,500 only to discover it on Myntra for ₹3,200 the next day. That ₹1,300 difference felt like a betrayal.

So we built SoleMate — a platform that aggregates real-time shoe prices from every major platform and puts them side-by-side. No more browser tabs. No more regret.

Today, over 10,000 customers use SoleMate to shop smarter, compare instantly, and always pay the lowest price.

Return & Exchange Policy

Simple. Transparent. Fair.

At SoleMate, we aim to ensure every customer receives the perfect fit. We follow an Exchange-Only Policy. We do not provide monetary refunds once a product has been purchased.

Do You Offer Refunds?
No, we do not offer monetary refunds.

Once a product has been purchased, we do not process cash refunds under any circumstance. However, we are committed to making it right — if you've received a wrong or defective product, we will exchange it for the correct item at no additional cost.
When Can I Exchange My Shoe?
We allow exchanges only in the following cases:

• Size Mismatch — The shoe you received doesn't match the size you ordered.
• Manufacturing Defects — Visible stitching errors, sole separation, or material defects present at time of delivery.
• Damaged Product — The product was damaged during shipping before reaching you.
Exchange Conditions
To qualify for an exchange, the following conditions must be met:

• Request must be made within 7 days of delivery.
• The shoe must be unused and in original condition.
• The original tag must remain attached.
• The product must not show signs of wear or customer-caused damage.
• Original packaging must be returned with the shoe.
How to Request an Exchange?
1. Contact our support team within 7 days via email at exchange@solemate.in or WhatsApp at +91 98765 43210.

2. Share your order ID, a brief description of the issue, and photos of the defect (if applicable).

3. Our team will review your request within 24–48 hours and provide you with a pickup slot or return instructions.

4. Upon receipt and inspection, we'll dispatch your replacement within 3–5 business days.
Important Notes
• All exchange requests are subject to physical inspection of the returned product.
• If the product does not meet the conditions listed above, the exchange request may be declined.
• Exchange is subject to product availability in the requested size/color.
• Sale items and limited edition drops are not eligible for exchange unless defective.
• For any disputes, our customer support team's decision will be final.
